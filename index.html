Our Synergistic Vision

Led by Prof. Dr. Alaa Abdallah El-Sadek, AFYS and EWB operate in synergy. AFYS generates the scientific innovation and empowers the human capital needed to develop groundbreaking solutions. EWB then provides the strategic framework, institutional support, and financing mechanisms to implement these solutions on a national scale, particularly in the vital water sector. Together, we translate science into societal impact.
