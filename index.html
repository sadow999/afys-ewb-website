Who We Are

The Arab Foundation of Young Scientists (AFYS-EWB), a public benefit foundation registered in Egypt, was established to enhance the voice of young scientists in decision-making and action for science and society. We work across Arab and African countries to encourage scientific research, increase the impact of scientific knowledge, and build a strong network for young scientists.
